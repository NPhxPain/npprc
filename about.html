About

Our Philosophy

Our unique model for providing patients with the highest quality of care possible involves two important components:

  • We utilize the most current technology and treatment protocols
  • Every patient receives direct care from our team of doctors, each with different specialties for treating their condition.

Our technology and treatment protocols are designed specifically for patients with severe back, neck, and disc pain, as well as muscle, joint and nerve pain. Many patients we successfully treat have been suffering with their condition for years, even after trying several different forms of therapy. If not diagnosed and treated early enough, many of these conditions lead to spinal surgeries.

Continue reading

The reason why most chronic back, neck, and disc pain sufferers still have their symptoms after so many years is because they either received no treatment at all, or the treatment they received was not designed to provide long-term stability and correction.

If we are able to accept you as a patient, you can feel confident that our team of doctors will be working together on your case to identify and address the root cause of your condition. In doing so, long-term stability can be achieved and future surgery can be avoided.

To find out if you are a candidate for our non-surgical procedures and treatment protocols, we invite you to schedule a consultation with one of our doctors.

We look forward to improving the long-term quality of your life.
