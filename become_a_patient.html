Insurance

Our facility accepts, and is in network with a multitude of major insurance plans. These plans include:

  • United HealthCare
  • Cigna
  • Aetna
  • Humana
  • BlueCross Blue Shield
  • Medicare

Prior to your first visit to our office, we will verify your health insurance benefits so that when you sit down to consult with the Clinic Director, your specific coverage will be explained. This allows you to make fully informed decisions about becoming a potential new patient at North Phoenix Pain Relief Center.

Financial Policies

Since your health insurance coverage is fully explained to you prior to starting care in our facility, there are absolutely no "financial" surprises. If your insurance plan includes office Co-Pays or a Deductible, we have absolutely no problem working with you in order to develop comfortable methods of payment throughout, or at the end of treatment.

We stand by the fact that a patient's financial obligations will not stand in the way of receiving the highest quality of health care that our facility has to offer.
